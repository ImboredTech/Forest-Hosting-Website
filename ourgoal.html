Our Goal

Our Main Goal

Our main goal is to cut down general carbon emissions by using net zero / close to net zero servers that still uphold to most standards the customers demand for.

Why are we doing it?

Climate change is an serious topic, and it keeps on getting pushed aside by world leaders. So I am trying to do my part to try and help cut down on all emissions that cause Climate Change.
